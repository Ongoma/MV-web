GLOBAL ELECTIVES PORT LTD

About

Global Electives Port organizes Medical, Engineering, Law, Teaching and Volunteer placements to countries like Kenya ,Philippines, Tanzania and Uganda for students interested in combining their electives with the experience of new lifestyle, culture, Engineering, Law, teaching and medical practice, and treatment in developing countries in Africa and Asia. As we immerse our students with the community, our staffs provide security and support to ensure their safety and welfare while in practice.

We have partnered with major hospitals and district clinics, Engineering, Schools, Law firms or a combination of both to provide our clients with invaluable insight and training in the different fields. We also provide accommodation in our guest houses across and within the towns where your placements are located. They will be housed together with the other students and volunteers in safe areas, some nearer to beaches, shopping malls and local shopping centers full of good social environment, living and experiencing the traditional way of life by extension.

Mission

Our mission is to transform the world through the infusion of Knowledge and culture.

Vission

Global Elective Port will be known for its high value and compassionate medical internship program by educating future health care staffs and leaders in the medical field. Enriching experiences in the practical setting, laboratories, and research can help them discover innovative ways to advance medical services worldwide.Visit Us At Global Electives Port?
